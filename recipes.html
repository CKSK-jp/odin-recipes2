Butter Chicken Poutine

Potatoe cravings? Indian food but don't want to visit India? Just make this butter chicken poutine at home! With these few ingredients, you'll have a michelin star level dish ready in to time!

Ingredients

  • Frozen cut fries
  • Instant butter chicken mix
  • Chicken cut into cubes
  • Cheese curds

Cooking Instructions

  1. Deep-fry the frozen fries in a fryer.
  2. Mix the instant butter chicken mix in a pan with the chicken cubes.
  3. Place the fries in a cup and drizzle it with the butter chicken sauce.
  4. Garnish the dish with cheese curds.
